Nonprofit Software License Agreement

PocketCache, LLC · Effective June 2026

Plain-English summary: This agreement is between PocketCache, LLC and the nonprofit organization ("Nonprofit") that signs up to use PocketCache's software. PocketCache provides the technology. The Nonprofit runs its own round-up giving program, collects donations on its own Stripe account, and issues tax receipts to donors. PocketCache charges a flat fee — never a percentage of donations.

1. License Grant

PocketCache, LLC ("PocketCache") grants the Nonprofit a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable license to use the PocketCache software platform (the "Platform") to operate a branded round-up giving program for the Nonprofit's own donors. This license does not include the right to sublicense, resell, or transfer access to the Platform to any third party.

2. Flat Fee Schedule

Software fee: $0.50/active linked user/month — billed monthly by invoice to the Nonprofit's billing account.

Processing fee: $0.50/charge per donor/month — collected from donors at checkout via a pre-checked opt-in. When a donor covers the fee, it is routed to PocketCache automatically via Stripe Connect application fee. When a donor opts out, the $0.50 is deducted from the round-up total before the Nonprofit receives it.

No setup fees. No percentage of donation amounts. Ever.

"Active linked user" means a donor who has at least one linked Plaid account and at least $0.01 in round-ups during the billing month.

3. Nonprofit Responsibilities

  • The Nonprofit is the soliciting party for all donations made through the Platform. PocketCache does not solicit contributions.
  • The Nonprofit must maintain all required charitable solicitation registrations in applicable states. PocketCache is not responsible for state registration compliance.
  • The Nonprofit must operate its own Stripe Connect Standard account and is the merchant of record for all donor charges. Stripe's Connected Account Agreement governs the Nonprofit's Stripe relationship.
  • The Nonprofit is responsible for issuing tax receipts and all donor communications. PocketCache does not issue tax receipts.
  • The Nonprofit must not represent PocketCache as a fundraiser, charitable organization, fiduciary, or holder of donation funds.
  • The Nonprofit must not use the Platform to solicit donations from California residents until PocketCache has confirmed California availability.

4. PocketCache Provides Technology Only

  • PocketCache provides software, infrastructure, and payment facilitation technology.
  • PocketCache is not a professional fundraiser, fundraising counsel, or commercial co-venturer as defined under any state charitable solicitation law.
  • PocketCache does not provide fundraising advice, strategy, or consulting.
  • PocketCache never receives, holds, controls, or has custody of donation funds. All charges are direct charges on the Nonprofit's Stripe Connect account.

5. Stripe Connect

The Nonprofit must connect its own Stripe account via Stripe Connect Standard OAuth. The Nonprofit accepts Stripe's Connected Account Agreement. PocketCache uses Stripe's application_fee_amount parameter per charge to collect the processing fee; this amount is explicitly disclosed to donors at checkout. PocketCache is not responsible for Stripe service interruptions, Stripe's terms changes, or Stripe's decisions regarding the Nonprofit's account.

6. Trademark Use

The Nonprofit may display "Powered by PocketCache" in its donor-facing materials in accordance with PocketCache's brand guidelines. The Nonprofit may not use PocketCache's name, logo, or marks in any way that implies PocketCache endorses the Nonprofit's fundraising activities, is a co-fundraiser, or is a registered charitable organization.

7. Branding and White-Label

PocketCache grants the Nonprofit the right to configure the Platform with the Nonprofit's own logo, colors, and story text to create a branded donor experience. All branding must comply with applicable law and must not be misleading. The Nonprofit is responsible for ensuring that all branding content it uploads is accurate and that the Nonprofit has the right to use it.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement on 30 days' written notice. Upon termination, PocketCache will wind down the Nonprofit's program and stop processing new donor enrollments. Existing active donors will be notified of the program's end. PocketCache will complete any charges already initiated before the termination date. The Nonprofit remains responsible for issuing tax receipts for all charges processed before termination.

PocketCache may terminate immediately upon written notice if the Nonprofit materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within 10 days of notice.

9. Limitation of Liability

PocketCache's liability to the Nonprofit for any claim arising from this Agreement or use of the Platform is limited to the fees paid by the Nonprofit to PocketCache in the 3 months preceding the claim. PocketCache is not liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages of any kind. PocketCache is not liable for actions or omissions of Stripe, Plaid, or any third-party service provider.

10. Arbitration

Any dispute arising from this Agreement that cannot be resolved informally will be resolved by binding arbitration administered by the American Arbitration Association under its Commercial Arbitration Rules. The arbitration will be conducted in Wisconsin. Both parties waive the right to a jury trial. Class arbitration is not permitted.
