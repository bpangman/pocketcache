7. Round-Up Donations

PocketCache monitors your linked card's transactions via Plaid and rounds each transaction up to the nearest dollar. The difference — your "round-up" — accumulates throughout the month. Once a month, the Nonprofit charges your payment method for your accumulated round-ups. The charge appears under the Nonprofit's name on your bank or card statement. The Nonprofit issues your tax receipt directly.

Accumulated round-ups below the Nonprofit's monthly minimum (default $10) are not charged that month; they roll over to the following month, and any applicable service fee rolls over with them. Regardless of the rollover minimum, a settle-up charge runs at least once every 3 calendar months, collecting all accrued round-ups and fees. Cancelling your account triggers a final settle-up charge for all accrued round-ups and fees at the time of cancellation.

8. Service Fee

PocketCache charges a flat $1.00/month service fee per active linked donor account. This fee is pre-selected as "I'll cover it" at checkout, which means the Nonprofit pays nothing for your account — the $1.00 covers both the $0.50 Stripe processing fee and the $0.50 Nonprofit software fee. If you opt out of covering the fee, $0.50 is deducted from your round-up total before the Nonprofit receives it, and PocketCache bills the Nonprofit $0.50/month for your account. The $1.00 service fee is not tax-deductible. Your round-up donation amount is tax-deductible as allowed by law. The Nonprofit issues all tax receipts.

9. ACH Payment Authorization

ACH Debit Authorization: By selecting bank account (ACH) as your payment method, you authorize the Nonprofit and its payment processor (Stripe) to initiate recurring monthly electronic debit entries to your designated bank account for your accumulated round-up total. If you have kept the pre-selected $1.00/month service fee, that amount will also be debited as a separate charge; if you opted out, no additional debit will be made from your account for the service fee (the $0.50 cost to you is instead deducted from your round-up total). This authorization will remain in effect until you cancel your account or change your payment method in the app. To revoke, update your payment method in Settings or contact support@pocketcache.app.

10. PocketCache's Fees to Nonprofits

When a donor keeps the pre-selected $1.00/month service fee, PocketCache charges the Nonprofit nothing for that donor — the donor's $1.00 covers both the processing fee and the software fee. When a donor opts out, PocketCache invoices the Nonprofit a flat $0.50/month for that donor (and $0.50 is also deducted from that donor's round-up total). These are flat fees unrelated to donation amounts — PocketCache never takes a percentage of donations.

If the retry fails, your account may be paused and you will be notified to update your payment method. Round-ups continue to accumulate during a payment pause. Service fees continue to accrue as well, and the regular 3-month settle-up schedule applies regardless of the pause.
