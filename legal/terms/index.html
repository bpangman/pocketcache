7. Round-Up Donations

PocketCache monitors your linked card's transactions via Plaid and rounds each transaction up to the nearest dollar. The difference — your "round-up" — accumulates throughout the month. Once a month, the Nonprofit charges your payment method for your accumulated round-ups. The charge appears under the Nonprofit's name on your bank or card statement. The Nonprofit issues your tax receipt directly.

PocketCache charges a flat $0.50/month processing fee per active linked donor account. At checkout, you will see a pre-checked option to cover this fee so that 100% of your round-ups go to the Nonprofit. If you opt out, the $0.50 fee is deducted from your round-up total before it is charged by the Nonprofit. The processing fee is not tax-deductible. Your donation amount is tax-deductible as allowed by law.
