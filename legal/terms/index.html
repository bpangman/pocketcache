7. Round-Up Donations

PocketCache monitors your linked card's transactions via Plaid and rounds each transaction up to the nearest dollar. The difference — your "round-up" — accumulates throughout the month. Once a month, the Nonprofit charges your payment method for your accumulated round-ups. The charge appears under the Nonprofit's name on your bank or card statement. The Nonprofit issues your tax receipt directly.

Accumulated round-ups below the Nonprofit's monthly minimum (default $5, configurable per nonprofit) are not charged that month; they roll over to the following month, and any applicable service fee rolls over with them. Regardless of the rollover minimum, a settle-up charge runs at least once every 3 calendar months, collecting all accrued round-ups and fees. If you cancel your account, you choose whether to send your accrued round-ups as a final donation or waive them (see Section 12).

8. Service Fee

PocketCache charges a flat, mandatory $1.00/month service fee for each calendar month in which your linked card generates round-ups. The fee is non-optional and is PocketCache's only revenue. In months where your balance rolls over below the minimum, the fee accrues and is collected together with your round-ups in the eventual single charge. This $1.00 fee is not tax-deductible and is never collected from the Nonprofit.

Separately, at checkout you will see a pre-checked option to cover the Nonprofit's card-processing costs (approximately 2.2% + $0.30 per monthly charge). This processing-cover amount: (a) is a voluntary donation to the Nonprofit, not a fee to PocketCache; (b) is tax-deductible as allowed by law; (c) passes through entirely to the Nonprofit — PocketCache receives none of it; and (d) causes the Nonprofit to net 100% of your round-ups for that billing cycle. If you uncheck this option, the Nonprofit receives your round-ups minus standard card-processing costs — the same outcome as any card donation to a nonprofit. The Nonprofit issues all tax receipts.
