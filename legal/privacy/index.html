Privacy Policy

PocketCache, LLC · Effective June 2026 · Last updated June 2026

Plain-English summary: We read your credit card transactions (via Plaid) only to calculate round-up amounts. We never move money from your tracked card - we only help the nonprofit charge your designated payment method once a month. We use Plaid for transaction data and Stripe for payment processing. We do not sell your data. Your bank credentials are never stored by PocketCache.

1. Who We Are

PocketCache, LLC ("PocketCache," "we," "us," or "our") is a software company that provides white-label round-up giving technology to nonprofit organizations. We are a technology vendor, not a fundraiser or charitable organization. Our contact email: privacy@pocketcache.app.

2. Information We Collect

Account information - your name, email address, state of residence, and account preferences you provide when you sign up.

Financial account data via Plaid - To calculate your round-ups, we use Plaid Technologies, Inc. ("Plaid") to connect to the credit or debit card you choose to track. Through Plaid, we access:

  • Transaction history (merchant name, date, amount) on your linked card
  • Account metadata (institution name, account type, last four digits)

We access this data solely to calculate your round-up amounts. We do not access your full account number, routing number, PIN, or login credentials. Plaid is a separate service subject to its own privacy policy - see Plaid's End User Privacy Policy. You may also visit my.plaid.com to manage or revoke PocketCache's access at any time.

Payment information - To collect your monthly round-ups, the Nonprofit uses Stripe, Inc. to process payments via bank account (ACH), Apple Pay, or card on their own Stripe Connect account. Stripe stores your payment credentials; PocketCache does not store card numbers or full bank account details. PocketCache never has custody of your payment credentials.

Usage data - Standard app interaction data (screens visited, features used) to improve the product. We do not track you across other apps or websites.

3. Plaid Data Use

We use Plaid Technologies, Inc. to link your financial accounts. Plaid provides read-only access to your transaction data for the sole purpose of calculating round-up amounts. Your bank credentials are never stored by PocketCache. Plaid's privacy policy governs their data use: plaid.com/legal/privacy. We do not sell your financial data.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate your daily round-up amounts from Plaid transaction data
  • Facilitate the Nonprofit's monthly charge of your payment method via Stripe
  • Provide the Nonprofit with the information it needs to issue tax receipts and donor communications
  • Notify you of failed payments or account issues
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Improve and maintain the app

We do not use your financial transaction data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose other than calculating your round-ups.

5. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as necessary to operate the service:

  • Plaid - to access your linked card's transaction data. Plaid's privacy policy.
  • Stripe - to process your monthly payment on behalf of the Nonprofit. Stripe's privacy policy.
  • The Nonprofit you have chosen - to operate the service, we always share your name, email address, and donation amounts with your Nonprofit so it can issue your tax receipts and administer your donations. Additionally, if you have opted in to data sharing (see Section 8 and Terms §4a), we share your contact information and giving activity (donation totals, round-up history, and account status) so your Nonprofit can include you in their donor newsletter, campaigns, and communications. In all cases, we share your data only with the specific Nonprofit you have enrolled with - never with other organizations, never with other PocketCache-enrolled nonprofits, and never sold. You may withdraw your data-sharing consent at any time in Settings › Notifications; withdrawal stops future newsletter/campaign sharing (it is prospective and does not require deletion of data already shared, and receipt-related sharing continues as necessary to operate the service). The Nonprofit’s own privacy policy governs their use of your information once received.
  • Legal compliance - if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and transaction data for as long as your account is active and for up to 7 years after account closure to comply with financial recordkeeping requirements. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8) - note that we may retain records required by law.

7. Security

We use industry-standard security practices: TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and access controls limiting who can view your data. Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe on the Nonprofit's Stripe Connect account. We never store raw card numbers or bank account credentials.

8. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access - you can download your data from the Settings screen in the app
  • Deletion - you can delete your account from Settings, which removes your personal data (subject to retention requirements above)
  • Revoke Plaid access - visit my.plaid.com to disconnect your linked card from Plaid at any time. This will stop future round-up calculations.
  • Opt out of marketing - you can manage email preferences in Settings
  • Questions or requests - email privacy@pocketcache.app

9. California

PocketCache's donor services are not currently available in California. California residents are not eligible to create donor accounts. We are not collecting personal information from California residents for the purpose of offering these services. This notice will be updated when California becomes supported.

10. Children's Privacy

PocketCache is not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us at privacy@pocketcache.app.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or in-app notification at least 30 days before the change takes effect. Continued use of PocketCache after changes constitutes acceptance.
