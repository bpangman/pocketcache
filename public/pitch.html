Revenue Model — No Middleman

Donations hit BGCA’s Stripe
directly. Period.

No DAF, no Endaoment, no Treasury float. PocketCache is a software vendor. BGCA is the charity. The money flows accordingly.

PocketCache charges BGCA a flat monthly invoice: $0.50/active linked donor/month. Donors can optionally cover a $0.50/month processing fee at checkout (pre-checked) — when they do, that fee routes to PocketCache automatically via Stripe’s application_fee. When they don’t, it’s deducted from the round-up before BGCA receives it. Either way, BGCA’s net cost is near $0.

Risk & Liability

What could go wrong?
Almost nothing lands on BGCA.

Money never touches PocketCache. BGCA owns its Stripe account and can disconnect us with one click. The regulated risk sits with Stripe and Plaid — by design.

💳
Money Never Touches PocketCache
Donations flow from the donor’s card → BGCA’s own Stripe account → BGCA’s bank. PocketCache never sees, holds, or moves donor funds.

BGCA Owns Its Stripe Account
Stripe Connect Standard means BGCA has full control. You can disconnect PocketCache with one click — no lock-in, no permission required from us.
