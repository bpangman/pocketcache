How It Works

Set it up once.
Your donors give every day.

PocketCache is software your nonprofit uses to run its own round-up giving program. No fundraising middleman, no percentage ever.

📄
Step 01

Enter Your EIN

IRS auto-verified in seconds. We confirm your 501(c)(3) status instantly, no paperwork required.

💳
Step 02

Connect Your Stripe

You’re the merchant of record. Donations charge directly on your own Stripe account — PocketCache never touches the money.

🔗
Step 03

Share Your Link

Donors join via QR code or link. No app download required. Share on your website, email campaigns, or club locations.

☕
Step 04

Donors Give Automatically

Round-ups charge monthly on your Stripe. You issue receipts. PocketCache invoices you a flat $0.50/active user/month — that’s it.
