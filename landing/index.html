How It Works

Set it up once.
Your donors give every day.

PocketCache is the software behind your program — you run it, you own it. No middleman, no percentage, ever.

💳
Step 02

Everything Included

A full giving platform,
not just an app.

App, web, widget, and dashboard — all built for how your donors actually give.

📱

App and web giving

Donors can give from a branded app or straight from the browser at pocketcache.app/YOURCODE/give — no app download needed.

🌐

Your own public micro-site

A public page at pocketcache.app/YOURCODE with your story, live impact stats, join buttons, and a shareable QR code.

🔗

One-line website widget

Drop a single line from pocketcache.app/widget.js into your existing site to add a “Round up for us” button anywhere.

📊

A full nonprofit dashboard

Overview, donor list, charge history, and growth tools in one place. Edit your branding and story anytime — sensitive changes require an emailed one-time code.

🎁

Extra gifts, matches, and volunteers

“Give Extra” one-time gifts, corporate match campaigns (match money flows straight from sponsor to nonprofit — PocketCache never touches it), and volunteer sign-up buttons.

🎯

Donor controls that reduce churn

A monthly giving cap, the ability to adjust a pending charge, skip-a-month, and cancel anytime — never a fee to leave.

🧩

Billing donors can trust

Round-ups accrue all month, the cycle locks on the 1st, donors are emailed the exact amount, and the charge runs on the 11th — a full 10 days’ notice, with a review window and one-time adjustment before it runs.

🔒

Security by design

Sign in with Apple or Google only — no passwords ever stored. Optional Face ID / Touch ID app lock. Bank connection is read-only via Plaid. Card data and payouts stay with Stripe, never PocketCache.

Simple, Flat Pricing

One flat fee.
Never a percentage.

No invoices for your nonprofit. Donors pay one small, itemized app fee — that’s our entire business model.

Your Nonprofit
$0 forever

Free to run your program — no invoices exist for your nonprofit, no contracts, and never a percentage of donations.

Your Donors
$1/month

A flat app fee, only for months they’re actively rounding up. It’s PocketCache’s only revenue — itemized right on their bill, and it rolls over along with any sub-minimum round-up balance.

  • ✓A pre-checked (opt-out, not opt-in) option lets donors also cover your card-processing costs, so your nonprofit nets 100% of round-ups if they leave it checked.
  • ✓Charges only fire once round-ups hit a $5 default monthly minimum (configurable per nonprofit) — balances under that roll over.
  • ✓A settle-up runs at least every 3 months regardless of whether the minimum was hit.
  • ✓PocketCache never takes a percentage of donations — not now, not ever.
